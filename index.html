Who are we

Kupambana is a Pan-Afric social movement organisation founded in 2018 at African Leadership University Rwanda, with an aim of creating love for our continent in the spirit of young educators. We are currently operating at ALU Rwanda, but we are planning to expand our activities to other campuses around African continent.

About us

Mission

creating African students with patriotic minds and spirits to work towards the greatness of African continent.

Vision

Vision to become the leading Pan-Afric social movements in university and highschools around the African continent.
